IMAGES FOLDER — What to put here
=================================

hero-bg.jpg       — Full-width background image for the hero section.
                    Ideal size: 1920×1080px or larger. Can be a lab photo,
                    workshop, machine, or any engineering scene you like.

profile.jpg       — Your circular profile/headshot photo.
                    Ideal size: 400×400px (square). Will be cropped to a circle.

about.jpg         — A second personal photo shown in the About section.
                    Ideal size: 420×480px (portrait orientation works best).

projects/
  project1.jpg    — Photo for project card 1. Ideal size: 800×500px.
  project2.jpg    — Photo for project card 2. Ideal size: 800×500px.
  project3.jpg    — Photo for project card 3. Ideal size: 800×500px.
  project4.jpg    — Photo for project card 4. Ideal size: 800×500px.

TIPS:
- JPG format is fine; WebP is smaller/faster if you want to optimize.
- Compress images at https://squoosh.app before uploading to keep the site fast.
- If an image is missing, the site still works — it just shows a grey placeholder.
